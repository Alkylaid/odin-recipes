Korean Beef Bowl

Description

Do you like beef?! Do you like rice?! Do you like Korean food?! Then this Korean Beef Bowl recipe is perfect for you.

I don't really know if this is what they eat in Korea, but it tastes delicious. It's quick and easy to make and does not require a lot of cleanup.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 5 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tablespoon freshly grated ginger
  • 2 teaspoons toasted sesame oil
  • 1/2 cup reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup light brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 6 green onions, chopped, divided
  • 4 cups hot cooked brown rice
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add beef and cook, stirring and crumbling into small pieces until browned, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain excess grease.
  2. Add garlic, ginger, and sesame oil, stirring until fragrant, about 2 minutes. Stir in soy sauce, brown sugar, and red pepper. Cook until some of the sauce abosrbs into the beef, about 7 minutes. Add 1/2 of the chopped green onions.
  3. Serve beef over hot cooked rice; garnished with sesame seeds and remaining green onions.
